Our Heritage

From a 20-Seat Dream to Hyderabad's Sensation

Founded in 2015 by visionary Head Chef Arjun Reddy and business entrepreneur Kavya Sharma, Royal Spice emerged from a modest 20-seat diner near Banjara Hills to become a legendary three-floor multi-cuisine masterpiece.

Driven by a passion to combine traditional Indian spices with sleek modern presentation, the founders spent months traveling across India—sourcing secret spice blends and ancestral recipes from Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, Punjab, and Rajasthan.

Today, we offer an elegant dining escape featuring dynamic family sections, ambient rooftop views, and premium private party setups, while remaining dedicated to hygiene and warm hospitality.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to basic questions about booking policies and rooftop dining.

Is rooftop dining available every day?

Yes! Our premium rooftop dining setup is open daily from 10:00 AM to 11:30 PM. However, access is subject to local weather and rain conditions for guest safety.

Is advance table booking required?

While walk-ins are welcome, advance booking is highly recommended on weekends (Friday evening through Sunday night) due to the heavy rush. Booking early secures your choice of rooftop or cabin sections.

Are birthday and anniversary decorations available?

Yes, we love celebrating with you! Birthday decorations, anniversary arrangements, and small custom event table setups are available upon request. Please fill out the special request section in our booking form or call our branch manager directly.

How can I place online food delivery orders?

You can easily place orders online through our partner platforms Swiggy and Zomato. Alternatively, you can call our phone line for home delivery or stop by the restaurant to pick up take-out packages directly.
