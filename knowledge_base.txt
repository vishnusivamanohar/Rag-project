Royal Spice Restaurant

Restaurant Overview:
Royal Spice Restaurant is a premium multi-cuisine restaurant established in 2015 in Hyderabad, India. The restaurant was founded by Chef Arjun Reddy and business entrepreneur Kavya Sharma. Their goal was to combine traditional Indian flavors with modern dining experiences. Over the years, Royal Spice became popular for its rich taste, hygienic cooking standards, family-friendly atmosphere, and affordable pricing.
Origin and History:the
Royal Spice started as a small family-owned restaurant with only 20 seats near Banjara Hills, Hyderabad. Due to its popularity and customer demand, the restaurant expanded into a three-floor dining space with private family sections, rooftop seating, and online delivery services. The founders traveled across India collecting authentic recipes from different states including Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Kerala, Punjab, and Rajasthan.
